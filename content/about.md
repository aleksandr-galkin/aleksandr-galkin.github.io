Senior Mechanical Engineer in the San Francisco Bay Area

I design robotics and precision systems that turn complex engineering challenges into manufacturable products. Over the course of my career in Germany and the U.S., I’ve led mechanical design work for companies such as Bosch, SPECS, and Serve Robotics, with a focus on performance, reliability, and efficient production.

My background spans patented lubrication technologies, precision instrumentation, and autonomous delivery systems. I’m driven by difficult problems, practical solutions, and products that work in the real world.
